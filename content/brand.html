Guidelines

The Name "Caddy"

The name of the software is Caddy. Please do not call it Caddy Server. If you wish to clarify the nature of the software, you may refer to it as the Caddy web server.

Non-endorsement

Do not use the logo to imply endorsement by the Caddy project. We provide it for promotional and media use only.

Presentation

Leave sufficient space (padding) around the image so it doesn't appear cramped or misplaced. Ensure placement does not hinder visibility or readability. Use images that contrast well with the background.

Do Not Modify

Please do not make changes to the image of the brand; keep colors, sizes, shapes, text, fonts, and other properties as-is. You may use the vector images as part of an illustration, but please attribute the illustration as a derivitive work of the original logo or image.

Size and Scale

The raster images are large enough to appear sharp on high-DPI screens. Do not increase the dimensions of the raster images or crop any of the images. Scale down only; keep original aspect ratios.

No Attribution Required

You are free to use these images (unchanged) on your web site without written permission or attribution.
